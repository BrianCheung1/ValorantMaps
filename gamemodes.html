VALORANT GAMEMODES

UNRATED

Unrated is where you can either play Valorant casually, or play to test your skill without worrying about your Valorant rank placement.

RANKED

Ranked Gamemode is where you play competitively against other player to get your placement in the Valorant ranks.

SPIKE RUSH

Spike Rush is a gamemode where you play a shortened version of the Plant/Defuse gamemode casually with other players. Games are first to 4 rounds won.

DEATH MATCH

Deathmatch is a game mode where you will be placed with 14 different players in a randomly selected map, to test your skill with the gun of your choice from the shop.

ESCALATION

Escalation is 5v5 game mode with 12 escalation levels (each level has a specific weapon or ability). The first team to 12, or furthest along in 10 minutes, wins.

Features

  • 5v5
  • 30-40 mins game time
  • 13 rounds to win
  • Can queue with any players
  • 100XP for round, 200XP for round win

Features

  • 5v5
  • 30-40 mins game time
  • 13 rounds to win/2 rounds extra in overtime
  • Diamond 3+ Solo/Duo only
  • Gain/Lose rank rating depending on game results/performance

Features

  • 5v5
  • 8-10 mins game time
  • Orbs for buffs and weapon upgrades
  • Random weapons each round
  • Pre-round time is reduced to 20 seconds (usually 30) and game time is reduced to 80 seconds (usually 100)

Features

  • 1v14
  • 9mins or 40 kills
  • Abilities are disabled as agents are chosen automatically.
  • Any weapon is purchasable
  • Health/Ammo packs are dropped on kills

Features

  • The team together must earn 7 points on a level to advance to the next level
  • A kill on the current level earns 1 point, a kill on a previous level earns .5 points
  • 7–9 mins
  • 800XP on a loss and 1000XP on a win
  • Level 1: Always either Raze’s Showstopper or Vandal/Phantom
  • Level 2: Always Vandal or Phantom.
  • Levels 3–11: Assorted weapons and abilities
  • Level 12: Possible Shorty, Classic, Knife, Shock Dart...or even the Snowball Launcher
